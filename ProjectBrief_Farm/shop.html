The Visitors Centre

Our centre serves as a little spot of confort on your journey but also a community centre with some activities for the rainy days.
The centre includes, a café, a little shop that sells local products and a little museum that follows the life of the people that worked this land before us.

Our Shop

Our lovely little shop sells local and handmade produce from our local community. Everything from flowers, trinkets and some of our more popular snacks.
We take pride in bringing light to our local artists and craftworkers so they may be enjoyed as much as everyone else.
